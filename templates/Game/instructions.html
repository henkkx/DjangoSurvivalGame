Houses

In the game, there are a number of houses or buildings. The player can choose to enter a house. Each of these houses has a number of different rooms.

Rooms

The rooms in the buildings may be occupied by other humans or creatures that may or may not be hostile. There are also various different items in the rooms, which can be picked up by the player.

Other humans/ creatures

The player may choose to talk to them or to fight them

Items

The rooms contain items such as weapongs or food. The player can pick up these items once he has defeated whatever monster occupied the room.
