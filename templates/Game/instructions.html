How the game works

Houses

In the game, there are a number of houses or buildings. The player can choose to enter a house. Each of these houses has a number of different rooms.

Rooms

The rooms in the buildings may be occupied by other humans or creatures that may or may not be hostile. Depending on the case, the player may choose to 1) talk to them and recruit them to the player's party or 2) to enter combat.
